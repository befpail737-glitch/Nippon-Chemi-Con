SMQ Series Snap-in Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitors

The SMQ series offers space-saving snap-in aluminum electrolytic capacitors with high capacitance density, ideal for industrial applications requiring significant capacitance in a compact form factor. These capacitors combine reliable performance with cost-effectiveness, making them perfect for general industrial applications that demand both electrical performance and economic value.

Performance Characteristics

High Capacitance Density

The SMQ series maximizes capacitance value in compact case sizes, making it ideal for applications where space is at a premium but substantial capacitance is needed.

Economic Performance

This series provides a well-balanced combination of electrical performance and cost efficiency, making it ideal for applications where value is as important as performance.

Snap-in Convenience

The snap-in mounting system provides secure mechanical connection while allowing for easier replacement during service compared to screw-terminal options.

Industrial Reliability

Despite the focus on economy, the SMQ series maintains the quality and reliability standards expected from NCC products in industrial environments.

Selection Guide

When selecting capacitors from the SMQ series, consider these key parameters:

Capacitance Requirements

The SMQ series offers high capacitance values in compact snap-in packages, ideal for applications requiring significant bulk capacitance where board space is constrained.

Thermal Considerations

While the SMQ series provides reliable performance, thermal management is important in high-power applications. Ensure adequate spacing and cooling to maximize operational life.

Mounting Configuration

The snap-in mounting allows for quick installation and serviceability. Consider the mechanical requirements and mounting force when designing your application.

FAE Note: The SMQ series is ideal for applications where space-saving is important but extreme ripple current handling is not the primary concern. For applications requiring maximum ripple capability, consider the KMQ series.
