Core Challenges in Industrial Applications

Harsh Environmental Conditions

Industrial drives operate in environments with high temperature, vibration, dust, and electromagnetic interference. NCC capacitors feature robust construction for these demanding conditions.

High Ripple Current Requirements

VFDs and servo drives generate significant ripple currents that require capacitors with high ripple tolerance to prevent overheating and degradation. NCC provides specialized solutions.

24/7 Operation Reliability

Industrial systems operate continuously with minimal downtime for maintenance. Our solution ensures long-life operation with high reliability to minimize unplanned maintenance.
