Our Story

Founded in 2008, LiTong Group began as a specialized distributor focusing on high-quality electronic components. Our partnership with Nippon Chemi-Con (NCC) started in 2010, recognizing NCC's reputation for excellence in aluminum electrolytic capacitors and other electronic components.

Over the years, we've grown from a regional distributor to a global partner, earning the trust of engineers and procurement professionals worldwide. Our success is built on a foundation of technical expertise, genuine products, and customer-focused service.

Today, we continue to expand our capabilities and service offerings, staying at the forefront of technology and market trends. Our team of experts combines deep technical knowledge with practical application experience to provide value-added solutions.
