Snap-in Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitors

NCC snap-in aluminum electrolytic capacitors feature a base self-standing design ideal for high-power applications requiring robust performance and easy mounting. With KMQ, SMQ and other specialized series, these capacitors offer higher capacitance values in compact footprints while maintaining reliable operation in demanding environments.

Specialized Series

KMQ, SMQ series for high-power applications

Voltage Range

6.3V to 500V options available

Capacitance Range

100µF to 150,000µF wide selection

Temperature Range

Standard: -40°C to +105°C
