Performance Characteristics

Enhanced Ripple Capability

The MVE series is specifically engineered for high ripple current applications with reduced ESR and enhanced thermal management characteristics compared to standard SMD series.

Extended Operational Life

Advanced electrolyte formulation and optimized internal construction provide 8,000-10,000 hour life ratings at +105°C for enhanced reliability in continuous operation applications.

SMT Reliability

Robust construction designed to withstand standard SMT assembly processes including reflow profiles and automated handling equipment.

Thermal Performance

Improved heat dissipation through optimized design allows for operation in higher ambient temperature environments typically found in industrial applications.

Selection Guide

When selecting capacitors from the MVE series, consider these key parameters:

Ripple Current Requirements

The MVE series excels in applications with high ripple current demands. For applications with lower ripple current requirements, consider the MVA series for cost optimization.

Thermal Management

While the MVE series is designed for high ripple applications, proper thermal design remains critical. Consider PCB copper area, thermal vias, and system cooling in your design.

Mounting Considerations

As SMD components, ensure appropriate PCB pad design and assembly processes match NCC's specifications for optimal mechanical and electrical performance.

FAE Note: The MVE series is optimal for industrial applications with high ripple current requirements where SMT assembly is preferred. For applications with standard ripple requirements, the MVA series provides an excellent cost-performance balance.
