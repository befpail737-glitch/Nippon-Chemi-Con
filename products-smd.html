SMD Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitors

NCC SMD aluminum electrolytic capacitors are designed for surface mount applications with space-saving benefits and reliable performance. Featuring MVA, MVE and other advanced series with optimized profiles for automated assembly and high-density mounting.

Standard Series

MVA, MVE series for general SMT applications

Voltage Range

6.3V to 450V options available

Capacitance Range

10µF to 10,000µF wide selection

Temperature Range

Standard: -40°C to +105°C

Featured Series

MVA Series

Standard SMD profile with excellent cost-performance ratio, 2000-8000 hours life at +105°C

✓ Low profile design

✓ High reliability

✓ Automated mounting

FAE Note: The MVA series is ideal for consumer electronics and general-purpose applications requiring SMD mounting.

MVE Series
