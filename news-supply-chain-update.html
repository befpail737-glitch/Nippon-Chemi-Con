Global Supply Chain Update: Improved Lead Times for NCC Capacitors

Published: October 8, 2024 by LiTong News Team Reading time: 8 minutes
Market Update Supply Chain Availability Lead Time

Based on current market conditions, we're seeing improved availability of NCC capacitors with lead times returning to normal levels for most series. This positive trend reflects stabilizing raw material prices, improved manufacturing capacity, and normalized global shipping conditions.

Current Market Conditions

After experiencing significant volatility in 2022-2023, the capacitor market is finally showing signs of stabilization. Nippon Chemi-Con (NCC) has successfully normalized production, and inventory levels at our facilities have returned to healthy levels for most product series.

Key improvements observed since July 2024:

  • Lead times for standard series have returned to 8-10 weeks
  • Increased production capacity at all NCC facilities
  • Stabilized raw material costs
  • Normalized shipping timelines globally

Customer Impact and Recommendations

The improved availability provides several benefits for our customers:

  • Reduced Project Delays: More predictable lead times enable better project scheduling
  • Inventory Optimization: Customers can reduce safety stock requirements
  • Cost Stability: Normalized supply conditions contribute to price stability
  • Design Confidence: Reliable availability supports confident product design decisions

For customers who experienced delays in 2023, we recommend reviewing your current designs and inventory strategies with our FAE team to take advantage of improved supply conditions.

Forward-Looking Statement

Based on current trends and NCC's production capacity, we anticipate stable availability through 2025. However, specific high-demand applications or extreme capacitance/voltage combinations may still experience longer lead times.

NCC continues to invest in both production capacity and R&D to support the growing demand in electric vehicle, renewable energy, and industrial automation markets.

"We're pleased to report that supply conditions have returned to normal for most NCC series. This allows our customers to resume standard ordering practices and reduces procurement risk in their supply chains." - LiTong Supply Chain Manager
