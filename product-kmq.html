The KMQ series represents NCC's solution for high-ripple current applications in industrial environments. Performance Characteristics

High Ripple Current Capability

The KMQ series is specifically engineered for applications with high ripple current requirements, utilizing optimized electrolyte formulations and enhanced internal construction to manage the thermal stress associated with high ripple conditions.

Extended Operational Life

With 8,000-10,000 hour life ratings at +105°C, the KMQ series provides reliable operation in applications requiring long-term performance without maintenance.

Mechanical Robustness

The snap-in mounting system provides secure mechanical connection while the robust internal construction resists vibration and shock commonly found in industrial environments.

Thermal Management

Enhanced thermal conductivity design facilitates heat dissipation, critical in high-ripple applications where internal heating is significant.

Selection Guide

When selecting capacitors from the KMQ series, consider these key parameters:

Ripple Current Derating

Account for ambient temperature and duty cycle when calculating ripple current derating. The KMQ series can handle significant ripple currents, but proper derating ensures extended life in critical applications.

Capacitance Selection

The KMQ series offers a wide range of capacitance values suitable for high-power applications where both capacitance and ripple handling are important. Consider voltage coefficient effects in your selection.

Mounting Considerations

The snap-in mounting method allows for easier replacement and serviceability compared to screw-terminal types, while still providing high capacitance values. Consider service requirements in your design.

FAE Note: The KMQ series is optimal for industrial applications with high ripple current requirements and where serviceability is important. For applications requiring maximum capacitance and voltage, consider the screw-terminal series.
