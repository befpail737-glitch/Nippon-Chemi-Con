NCC Receives ISO 14001 Certification for Environmental Management

Published: Aug 28, 2024 by LiTong News Team Reading time: 3 minutes

Nippon Chemi-Con (NCC) has officially received ISO 14001:2015 certification for its environmental management systems, marking a significant milestone in the company's commitment to sustainable manufacturing practices and environmental stewardship across all production facilities.

Environmental Certification Sustainability

Strengthening Environmental Commitment

The ISO 14001:2015 certification recognizes NCC's systematic approach to managing environmental responsibilities and demonstrates continuous improvement in environmental performance. This certification applies to NCC's manufacturing processes for aluminum electrolytic capacitors, ensuring that environmental impacts are minimized throughout the production cycle.

"Receiving ISO 14001 certification reflects our ongoing dedication to environmental responsibility," said Hiroshi Tanaka, Executive Officer for Sustainability at Nippon Chemi-Con. "This achievement validates our commitment to reducing environmental impact while maintaining the high-quality standards our customers expect from NCC products."

Certification Scope and Achievements

The certification covers NCC's primary manufacturing facilities and encompasses:

  • Waste reduction and recycling programs
  • Energy consumption optimization
  • Emissions monitoring and control
  • Water usage management
  • Supply chain environmental requirements

Key achievements during the certification process included a 15% reduction in energy consumption per unit of production and implementation of closed-loop water systems that reduced freshwater consumption by 25%.

Impact on Product Quality

The environmental management system enhancements required for ISO 14001 certification have also led to improvements in manufacturing consistency and quality control. Better environmental controls in production areas have contributed to more stable product characteristics and reduced variability.

All NCC products manufactured under the certified environmental management system continue to maintain the same high standards for electrical performance, reliability, and longevity that customers have come to expect.

Future Environmental Initiatives

NCC plans to expand its environmental initiatives with goals to achieve carbon neutrality at major facilities by 2030. Future plans include:

  • Installation of renewable energy systems
  • Implementation of circular economy principles in manufacturing
  • Development of eco-friendly product packaging
  • Expansion of recycling programs for end-of-life products

Customers can expect continued improvements in the environmental footprint of NCC products while maintaining the performance and reliability that have made NCC a global leader in electrolytic capacitor technology.

FAE Note: The ISO 14001 certification adds another level of assurance for customers concerned about environmental impact and corporate responsibility. NCC's commitment to sustainability aligns with growing regulatory requirements and customer expectations in environmentally conscious markets.
