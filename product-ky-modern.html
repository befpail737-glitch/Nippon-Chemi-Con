Selection Guide

When selecting capacitors from the KY series, consider these key parameters:

Ripple Current Requirements +

The KY series excels in applications with high ripple current demands. For applications with lower ripple current requirements, consider the KZE series for cost optimization.

Thermal Management +

While the KY series has enhanced thermal performance, proper thermal design remains critical in high-power applications. Consider PCB copper area, thermal vias, and system cooling in your design.

Life Expectancy Calculations +

Use NCC's life prediction models to determine actual operational life under your specific conditions. The 8,000-10,000 hour rating is at rated temperature and voltage.

FAE Note: The KY series is optimal for industrial applications with high ripple current requirements. For consumer applications with modest ripple demands, the KZE series offers better cost-effectiveness.
