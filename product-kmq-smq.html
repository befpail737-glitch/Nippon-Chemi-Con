Performance Characteristics

The KMQ series is specifically engineered for applications with high ripple current demands, utilizing advanced electrolyte formulations and optimized internal construction for enhanced reliability.

Mechanical Robustness

Snap-in mounting system provides secure mechanical connection, enhancing resistance to vibration and thermal cycling compared to screw-terminal solutions.

Selection Guide: KMQ vs SMQ

When choosing between KMQ and SMQ series, consider these key differences:

When to Choose KMQ Series +
  • High ripple current applications (>1 Arms)
  • Extended life requirements (15+ year systems)
  • Automotive and industrial applications with vibration stress
  • Applications requiring maximum reliability
  • High-temperature environments
When to Choose SMQ Series +
  • Standard ripple current applications
  • Cost-sensitive applications with less demanding requirements
  • General industrial equipment
  • Applications with standard life requirements
  • When price-performance ratio is a priority

FAE Note: The snap-in mounting configuration provides excellent mechanical stability and ease of field replacement compared to screw-terminal options. For applications with extreme vibration requirements, consider additional mechanical securing. For high-ripple applications, the KMQ series offers superior performance with enhanced ripple handling and extended life ratings.
