Enhanced longevity for LED lighting systems with extended operational life. Our NCC capacitor solution ensures consistent light output, reduced maintenance costs, and reliable operation for commercial and industrial LED lighting applications.

Core Challenges in LED Applications

Life Matching Requirements

LEDs typically last 50,000+ hours, while capacitors often fail much earlier, creating a system reliability bottleneck. NCC provides long-life capacitor solutions to match LED lifetimes.

High Ambient Temperatures

LED drivers in enclosed fixtures experience elevated temperatures that accelerate capacitor aging. NCC capacitors offer enhanced temperature performance for these applications.

High Ripple Current Density

LED drivers operate at high frequency with significant ripple current, requiring capacitors with low ESR and high ripple tolerance to minimize heating.

Implementation Guidelines

Step 1: Define LED System Requirements

Identify required lifetime, operating temperature, power rating, and dimming requirements for the LED fixture.

Step 2: Calculate Thermal Conditions

Determine the expected capacitor temperature based on thermal design and ambient conditions.

Step 3: Select Long-life Components

Choose NCC capacitors with appropriate life ratings and temperature characteristics for the application.

Step 4: Validate Life Expectancy

Perform life calculations based on actual application conditions and perform accelerated life testing.
