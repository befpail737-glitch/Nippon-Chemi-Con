Technical Questions

Capacitor lifespan roughly halves for every 10°C increase in operating temperature. NCC capacitors use advanced electrolyte formulations to extend life, but proper thermal management and derating are critical for applications at elevated temperatures.

Equivalent Series Resistance (ESR) directly impacts power dissipation and efficiency. High ESR can cause excessive heating and reduce efficiency, especially in high-frequency applications. NCC capacitors offer low ESR options for demanding applications.

Handle with ESD precautions, observe polarity for electrolytic types, and avoid mechanical stress. Store in dry, temperature-controlled environments. Refer to specific series data sheets for detailed handling instructions.
