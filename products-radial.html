Radial Lead Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitors

NCC radial lead aluminum electrolytic capacitors offer excellent performance for general applications. These capacitors feature KZE, KY, KMG and other classical series with reliable operation and long lifespan. Our inventory includes a wide range of capacitance values and voltage ratings to meet your design requirements.

Standard Series

KZE, KY, KMG series for general-purpose applications

Voltage Range

6.3V to 500V options available

Capacitance Range

1µF to 100,000µF wide selection

Temperature Range

Standard: -40°C to +105°C

Featured Series

KZE Series

General purpose with high reliability, 2000-8000 hours life at +105°C, standard ripple current capability

✓ Cost-effective solution

✓ Long operational life

✓ Wide voltage range

FAE Note: The KZE series is the go-to choice for switch-mode power supply output filtering due to its excellent cost-performance ratio.

Request KZE Series Datasheet

KY Series

High-ripple current capability, 8000-10000 hours life at +105°C, superior ripple handling

✓ High ripple tolerance

✓ Extended life

✓ Enhanced performance

FAE Note: Choose the KY series for applications with high ripple current requirements such as industrial power supplies.

Request KY Series Datasheet

KMG Series

Miniature profile with high capacitance, 2000-8000 hours life at +105°C, space-saving design

✓ Compact size

✓ High capacitance density

✓ Surface mount compatible

FAE Note: The KMG series is ideal for applications where board space is at a premium.
