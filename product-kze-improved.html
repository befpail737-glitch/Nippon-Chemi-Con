KZE Series Radial Lead Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitors

The KZE series represents NCC's general purpose radial lead aluminum electrolytic capacitors with high reliability, offering an excellent cost-performance ratio for a wide range of applications. These capacitors deliver dependable performance in switching power supply output filtering and other general-purpose applications.

Selection Guide

When selecting components from the KZE series, consider these key parameters:

Capacitance Requirements

The KZE series offers a wide range of capacitance values suitable for general filtering applications. Consider both nominal value and tolerance when selecting.

Voltage Derating

NCC recommends voltage derating of at least 20% for optimal life and performance under normal operating conditions.

Temperature Considerations

For applications with elevated ambient temperatures, consider the effect on life expectancy and select appropriate derating factors.

FAE Note: The KZE series is ideal for standard applications where a balance of performance and cost is required. For applications with higher ripple current requirements, consider the KY series.
